MULTI COLOR PRINTED CUSTOMIZED TAPES

We are dealing with multi-color/ printed /customized tapes which are extensively used for sealing medium to heavy-duty carton sealing, inventory management as well as in logistics industries. These tapes are widely used in the market and are known for their clearness and greater rigidity. We take custom orders as per our client’s requirements. We provide these tapes in different packaging options to meet clients’ needs.

The Primary Features of Multi Colour Printed Customized Tapes are:
  • Yash Pack Brand
  • Multi Color Tape Upto 8 Colours Printing
  • High Clarity
  • Great Rigidity
  • Durable - Non Ageing
  • High Shear Strength
  • Good Adhesive Strength
  • High Tensile Strength
